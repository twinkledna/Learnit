How to tie shoelaces

By Li

There are many ways to tie your shoes, and some may be easier than others. Whether you are trying to teach a child how to tie their shoes, or you're looking for a new technique, all you’ll need is a pair of patient hands and your favorite pair of shoes. This article will teach you how to tie your shoelaces using the "bunny ear" method

Materials

• Shoe

• Shoe Laces

Step 1:

Cross the laces

Take one lace over the other and pull tight.

Step 2:

Cross the laces

Take one lace over the other and pull tight.

Step 3:

Make one loop (“bunny ear”)

Fold one lace back on itself to form a loop. Hold it between your thumb and finger.

Step 4:

Push through to form a second loop

Tuck a bit of the wrapped lace through the hole behind the first loop to make another loop.

Step 5:

Pull both loops tight

Grab both loops and pull outward until the knot is snug.

Question

What's the best way to store slime?

Community Answer

Slimes tend to expand after play, so you can store any leftover portions in an airtight container, like Tupperware or a Ziploc bag. That prevents air exposure, which dries it out. Once the slime settles back to its original size after a few days, you can return the leftovers to the original jar it came it. Always keep it sealed to avoid drying out. If it does harden, a bit of vinegar can help revive it.
