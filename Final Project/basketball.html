How to Inflate a Basketball

By Li

Playing basketball with a flat ball decreases your game and stunts training. Playing basketball with too much bounce can also be damaging to the ball. For the best possible basketball, you’ll need to inflate it to the proper tension and care for it. There are alternatives if you do not have a ball pump.

Materials

• Basketball

• Air Pump / Bike pump

• Ball needle

Step 1:

Buy a decent air pump for your ball.

‎

‎Pumps aren't too expensive and are easy to find.

‎

‎You can even use a bike pump with the proper needle.

Step 2:

You’ll need to purchase a ball needle if your pump doesn’t already have one

Step 3:

Connect your needle to the pump.

‎

If you're using a bike pump, close the back tab on the pump before connecting it.

‎

Step 4:

Moisten the needle with water and insert it into the hole of the ball.

‎

Step 5:

The hole where you insert the needle is a small black rubber circle with a smaller hole.

Step 6:

Then pump up the ball to your preferred air level

Question

What's a basketball?

Community Answer

A basketball is an inflated, spherical ball used in the team sport of the same name. It is typically made of leather, rubber, or synthetic composite materials and features a pebbled texture and bounce capabilities that allow players to dribble, pass, and shoot the ball into the opponent's elevated basket.
