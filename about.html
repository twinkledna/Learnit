About Learnit

Welcome to Learnit - a place where learning how to do things is just as fun as doing them!

We create step by step guides designed especially for kids, using colorful rotoscoped animations that bring every lesson to life. Whether it’s drawing a cartoon, tying your shoes, building a simple project, or trying something totally new, we believe learning should feel like an adventure—not a chore.

Behind every guide is a team that cares about making learning simple, safe, and exciting. Our team involves educators, artists, and subject experts to make sure each lesson is accurate, easy to follow, and appropriate for young learners.

From the first idea to the final animation, every step is carefully crafted. Our experts break down skills into clear, manageable actions, then bring them to life through rotoscoped visuals that show exactly what to do

We also review and update our content regularly, so kids (and parents) can trust our guides to be helpful, current, and easy to understand. Our goal is to create a space where curiosity grows, confidence builds, and learning something new always feels possible.
